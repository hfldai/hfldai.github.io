I am a DPhil (PhD) student in Interdisciplinary Bioscience at University of Oxford under the supervision of Gerton Lunter. My research is about using statistical and computational methods to understand non-coding functional DNA and gene regulatory network from next-generation sequencing data. Specifically, I am developing statistical models to improve the interpretation of bulk and single-cell chromatin accessibility data and exploring the causal relationship between cis-regulatory elements within the genomic regulatory network.

I also contribute heavily in collaboration projects.

I work at Centre for Computational Biology of MRC Weatherall Institute of Molecular Medicine.

Outside of research, I enjoy creating some random pieces and covers on the piano.
